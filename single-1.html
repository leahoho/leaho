Nowadays, many women because of the need to attend a different occasion will buy different types of dress to meet the event. Or will be wearing a different kind of wedding dress in the wedding. It is no second chance to wear it again, but because of good memories, the dress will be well preserved. As the dress may not be able to repeat wear, the concept it's to re-design the dress can wear into daily life. Or rechange into other items, such as a pillow, bed sheets, and daughter’s dress. Not only can keep the dress and those meaningful memories, but also can enhance environmental awareness, no longer a waste of clothes.

“Upcycland” is a platform can looking for your favorite designers to help you re-design your memorable clothes, also can buy our brand re-designed products.
