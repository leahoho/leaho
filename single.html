AIA Doctor

UI/UX Deisgn

This is an App platform to providing client can easily search for AIA's network doctor. The client can search through the classification with the needs to search the doctor. Also, can use this App to real-time booking doctor, it's convenient and efficient to use of AIA's medical services. In addition, there is also a search function for nearby doctors, when client sick or critical, do not worry about finding a doctor difficult, just a button you can quickly know the doctor's location.

The App is hoping to solve a customer through a platform in the sick, no longer have the extra time to search for a doctor, to take care of the needs of the client.
